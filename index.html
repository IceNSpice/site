ABOUT US


We at ICE ‘N’ SPICE take this opportunity to introduce ourselves as a part of AJG Group, 40 year old enterprise having interest in Hospitality, Mining, Construction, Logistics, Agriculture industry and Social Service. We also run a Non-Governmental Organisation named Treasure of Kindness.


  • We currently operate three Ice 'n Spice cafes in Goa, one at Bits Pilani, the other at INS-Hansa Navy and HPCL.
  • Additionally, we have two additional outlets in Cochin that operate under the name and branding "bake my day."

We are a modern fast food cafe that believes in giving customers the best value for their money by offering high-quality food products, quick and amiable service, and a modern, hygienic environment.
Ice 'n' Spice is a rapidly expanding company led by Mr. Joge George, a health inspector with a keen eye for detail and a thorough understanding of the food industry.
Our motto is quick service with a smile & our vision to have a brand chain of outlets serving exclusive and international standard of cuisine.
